How to Play

  • Roll dice
  • What you roll is added to "current" score
  • If either of the dice roll a 1, current score goes to 0 and you lose your turn
  • If you press 'hold' during your turn, your current score becomes your accumulated score
  • First player to 100 accumulated points wins!
